Hard Disk Power Manager is a small utility to change the power management and performance settings of hard disks.
It offers settings to save power, reduce noise or increase performance. 
It solves problem with clicking noise (problem for many laptops).

Options
-------
Low Power - minimum power consumption with standby allowed,
Standby Allowed - maximum power consumption with standby allowed,
Standby Not Allowed - minimum power consumption with standby not allowed,
High Performance - maximum power consumption with standby not allowed.
